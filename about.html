My Hackathon Achievement

I am proud to have participated in the Arabh 2022 Hackathon in Kanpur, where my team and I secured the third place award in the ideation round. Our innovative idea addressed the pressing issue of plastic waste in today's world, which poses a significant threat to our environment. We conceptualized a machine that collects plastic bottles from users and, in exchange, provides them with coupons and coins. This initiative aims to encourage recycling and reduce plastic pollution.
